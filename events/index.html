Fun Facts about Severance

- The idea for *Severance* originated from the creators' fascination with work-life balance and technological impact on personal lives.

- The unique visual style was achieved using specific color palettes and lighting techniques to enhance the show's themes.

"The show has sparked conversations about the nature of work and personal identity in the modern world."
